Quality Assurance and Accessibility

All patterns, functionality and layouts are tested against our supported browsers. We also look for accessibility issues via an automated scan and with other assistive technology tools and methods. TMP is committed to the accessibility of it's websites and products and seeks to meet as much of WCAG 2.0 Level A and AA as possible. If you discover any problems with our work, please open up an issue and let us know about it or contact Michael Spellacy.
